Stuffed Peppers

Both you and the Peppers will be stuffed!

What you will need

  • Long grain white rice
  • Bell Peppers
  • Olive Oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Yellow Onion
  • Fresh Galic
  • Canned Petite diced tomatoes
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  2. Meanwhile, trim about 1/4-inch from tops of bell peppers and then remove stems, ribs and seeds. Fill a baking dish just large enough to fit peppers with about 1/2-inch of water.
  3. Place peppers upside down in water, cover tightly with foil and bake 20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile heat olive oil in a lage non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add onion and saute 3 minutes. Move onions to far side of the skillet.
  6. Add beef in chunks, season with salt and pepper then let sear until browned on bottom, 2-3 minutes
  7. Break up beef and toss with onions and continue to cook 2 minutes, add garlic and cook until beef is cooked through about 1 minute longer.
  8. Remove from heat, drain off excess fat (I like to tilt skillet to one side and dab up excess fat using tongs and paper towels).
  9. Stir in tomatoes, half of the tomato sauce (about 1/2 cup), cooked rice, parsley, Italian seasoning and season with salt and pepper to taste.

How Long To Cook Stuffed Peppers

  1. Reduce oven temperature to 350. Turn par-baked peppers upright and fill with beef filling.
  2. Pour remaining tomato sauce over peppers. Cover with foil and continue to bake 20 minutes.
