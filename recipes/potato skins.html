Crispy Potato Skins

Crispy potato skins you are going to love

Oh so crispy!

  • Small Russet Potatoes
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Bacon bits
  • Chives

Directions

  1. Choose smaller potates to make bite sized snack.
  2. Use russet potatoes and bake potatoes in oven until tender.
  3. Cool completely before cutting.
  4. Cut lenghtwise and use a spoon to scoop out the flesh. Leave about 1/4" of potato in the shell.
  5. Fill the potatoes with you favorite toppings.
